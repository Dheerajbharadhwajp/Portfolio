About School

The Poornaprajna Education Centre is run under the aegis of Udupi Sri Admar Mutt Education Council. The Founder President H.H.Sri Vibudhesha Theertha Swamiji, Pontiff of the Admar Mutt, has been a source of inspiration and guidance.It runs classes from Nursery to Standard VII in its Nursery and Primary sections; VIII to X Standard in the High School section. The Centre prepares student for the SSLC(Matriculation) Examination.The school is located in a beautiful locality with a large playground. It is a co-educational, English Medium Institution, following the state pattern. It has the necessary instructional Laboratories, well equipped Library, Reading Room, Computer Lab and Vocational Training Facilities.
